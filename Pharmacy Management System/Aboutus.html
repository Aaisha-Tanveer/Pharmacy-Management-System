About Us

Healthcare providers are continually looking to improve patient care experiences, and e-prescription is one great way of enhancing patient safety. E-prescription systems help healthcare providers to create and send prescriptions electronically to pharmacies for processing and delivery. We develop customized pharmacy management software architectures that maintain secure communication of e-prescriptions between the patients, their relative pharmacies, and claims agencies. The e-prescribing software simplifies the entire process flow of the pharmacy’s system, and error-free dispensing and billing increases patient satisfaction.
